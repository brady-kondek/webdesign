About

Brady Kondek is the Head Website Designer. He is in charge of designing and creating the websites. He has been making websites since June 2017, when he taught himself how to do so. He has also been using computers ever since he was 5 years old. At that time, he only knew how to open Internet Explorer, and navigate to a website. But over time and much dedication to computers, he has learned much more and is now able to make amazing things using computers.



Timeline

  • Learned how to use a computer in 2010
  • Started making Wix/Weebly sites in December 2016
  • Started making WordPress sites in October 2017
  • Learned HTML in June 2018
  • Started making HTML sites in Summer 2019
  • Created Brady Kondek Website Design in September 2019
